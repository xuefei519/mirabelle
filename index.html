Who is Mirabelle?

Mirabelle is a 2-year-old female cat, probably a Russian Blue mix. She came to the family in February 2015, now she is a principal member of the family.

Mirabelle is not just another cat, she is curious, cautious and active, she loves to play and go out. She is dependent and needs personal space as well. She is a keeper.

Tsing Gu

Tsing is a true cat lover, he has shown a great affection for cats since a very young age, or I would rather say that his liking for them is more like an instinct.
Unlike those who are attracted only by the cute appearance, Tsing's affection is sincere. Every day he spends time playing with Mirabelle, he tries to understand her behaviors, her feelings, and her needs. He makes sure that everything is good for her: he cleans the toilet every single morning, he always keeps an eye on her bowl in case it is going to be empty soon, and most important, he respects her nature and never forces her to do anything against her will.
If you have a pet cat then you must know that pain is inevitable, Tsing is no exception, he got all these scratches while playing with Mirabelle or trying to trim her nails. Those scratches, however painful they were, never get in the way of his love, his pure, gentle and unconditional love.

Tsing是一个真正的爱猫人士。他真的是一直都超级喜欢猫的，而且他不是只爱它们呆萌的样子，他是真的打心眼里关心它们。
他每天都会花一段时间逗Mirabelle玩，想要去深度地了解她的感受和她的需要。他对她照顾得很周全，每天早上他都会给Mirabelle铲屎，他还总是留意她的食盆是不是要空了，最重要的是他对她有起码的尊重， 他从来不会强迫她做她不愿意做的事。
养过猫的人都知道，被抓伤都是家常便饭，Tsing也不例外，他和Mirabelle玩或者是给她剪指甲的时候也经常挂彩。但是即便如此，他也从来没有怪罪过Mirabelle，这大抵就是爱吧。

Mirabelle

Mirabelle the cat, aka 'Mimi', considers herself as the master of the family. It is true that she is the one that stays at home for the most of the time, and she is familiar with every single corner, especially those the other two cannot reach to.
Mirabelle has zero tolerance for strangers, she sees them as 'intruders', for those of her size, she throws hisses at them; and for those of much bigger size, she would hide from them. But once you get to know her (which requires much time), she would disguise herself as a cute and innocent cat.
Just like other cats, Mirabelle has a crazy fondness for warm sunshine, every afternoon she lies on the carpet let the sunshine cover her whole body except her head, which is always beneath the sofa. She has her own way of weird, aren't we all? She enjoys being alone and yet not hiding the fact that she needs company sometimes as well. She never hides her desire or fakes anything, she is true to herself, deep down in her heart, she never forgets that she is a predator and was born to rule.

Mirabelle，昵称“咪咪”，认为自己是这个家里的霸主。不过她的确是在家里待的最久的那个，所以她对家里每个边边角角都特变熟悉，尤其是别人够不着的角角落落。
Mirabelle真的是完全不能忍受陌生人，她认为他们是”侵略者“，她会冲那些她体型相近的哈气，大个儿点的她就会避而不见。但是一旦你跟她混熟了（这可能要很久），你会发现她也不过是一只单纯可爱的小猫猫。
和别的猫一样，Mirabelle也特别喜欢晒太阳。每天下午她会躺在地毯上，尽情让太阳晒她的身子，只剩脑袋藏在太阳晒不到的沙发下面。她的确有点怪，但我们不都是这样吗？她喜欢独处也需要陪伴，对此她也毫不隐藏。她不遮掩自己的欲望也不懂得虚伪， 她活的很真实，她从未忘记自己捕食者的身份。

Shino Shi

Even though after all these years living with Mirabelle the cat, this girl still considers herself as a dog person. For a girl sensitive and insecure as Shino is, there is nothing wrong for her to want a pet to be loyal and supportive.
However, Mirabelle would never come to this family if it were not Shino who contacted her former-owners (a nice couple but sadly the boyfriend was suffering from allergy) for adoption. She tried to develop a close relationship with Mirabelle at the very beginning, but before long did she realize that things didn't work out the way she wanted: a cat can never be a dog, at least not for Mirabelle.
Currently, these two girls are keeping a healthy relationship, Shino has been holding back the urge to physically touch or binding emotionally with Mirabelle. And she is the one with clear mind and tries to prevent Tsing from spoiling Mirabelle, she made rules like 'No balcony activities after sundown', 'Catmint once a week maximum' and etc. She voluntarily plays the 'bad cop' just so she could protect Mirabelle even though she knows this can make the cat like her less. And nobody says it is easy.

哪怕是Mirabelle一起生活了这么多年，这个人还是觉得自己喜欢的是狗。不过作为一个敏感又没有安全感的少女来说，她想养只忠心又能提供陪伴的宠物也无可厚非
但是要不是有Shino， Mirabelle也不可能来到这个家里，当初是因为她联系了Mirabelle以前的主人（一对超好人的情侣，可惜男朋友过敏）才能领养了她。一开始Shino想要和Mirabelle培养一份亲密的关系，但是很快她就发现事情完全不会按照 她希望的方向发展，猫就是猫啊，怎么也变不成狗，至少Mirabelle不会。
现在这二位的关系还算健康，Shino一直压抑着自己想要抚摸Mirabelle和跟她情感交流的冲动。并且她是这个家里比较理智的那个人，她一直试图阻止Tsing过度宠溺Mirabelle，她定下例如”天黑之后不能在阳台玩“和”猫薄荷一周最多一次“等等规矩。 为了保护Mirabelle，她自愿在家里扮演红脸，即使她知道Mirabelle会因此更不喜欢她。

Friends

Noob

Noob

Mirabelle first met Noob at the end of April 2015, when his owners left for vacation and asked Tsing and Shino to take care of him temporarily. Noob is about 6 months younger than Mirabella, breed unclear, he was adopted at a very young age and grew up with mankind ever since. Most people say that Noob is a dog trapped in a cat, he likes to follow people and Mirabelle (like a stalker, which always upset her) and he never holds a grudge when Mirabelle hisses at him or even punches him.
Different from Mirabelle who is quite picky at food, Noob eats almost everything, literally EVERYTHING. Guess that's the exact reason why he gains weight so quick.
Noob is not the only cat Mirabelle has ever met, but probably the only cat so far she can get along well with.

Mirabelle第一次见到Noob是在2015的四月底，那个时候他的主人出门旅游暂时拜托Tsing和Shino代为照顾他。Noob大概比Mirabelle小六个月，品种不详，他很小的时候就被收养了，之后一直和人一起生活。见过Noob的人都觉得他是一只 狗性格的猫，他喜欢到处跟在人或者Mirabelle后面跑（像个跟踪狂，Mirabelle超烦他），而且就算Mirabelle冲他哈气或者甚至是打他，他也完全不会记仇。
Mirabelle是一只挑食到难以置信的地步的猫，但是Noob跟她截然相反，他什么都吃，真的是什么都吃。也许这就是他在持续发胖的原因罢。
Noob并不是Mirabelle唯一见过的猫，但是大概是她目前为止唯一能够和平相处的猫。
